Screens & product story

A simple loop designed for repeat outdoor use.

TrailLayers is organized around the same sequence outdoor athletes already follow mentally: know your gear, combine it for the day, then learn from the outcome.

Catalog the kit you actually own

Add photos, categories, insulation, sleeve length, fit, waterproofing, retailer details, and size data in one place.

Build repeatable outfit systems

Group garments into saved outfit presets so cold starts, shoulder-season rides, and fast weather changes are easier to plan for.

Turn each activity into feedback

Track what you wore, the temperature, effort, and comfort so your next decision comes from evidence, not vague memory.

About TrailLayers

The app is built around a gap other outdoor platforms ignore but directly affects your comfort and performance.

Route apps help you choose where to go. Training apps help you measure what you did. TrailLayers focuses on the gear layer: what you wore, how it handled the conditions, and what deserves a place in your rotation.

The goal is not generic outfit inspiration. It is practical memory for people who train and travel outdoors often enough that small layering decisions change comfort, pace, and confidence.
