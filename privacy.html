Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

TrailLayers is an iOS app for tracking outdoor gear, building outfits, and logging activity performance. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how you can control it.

What we collect

  • Email address — used solely to create and authenticate your account.
  • User-created content — garments, outfits, activity logs, comfort ratings, and weather notes that you enter manually.
  • Garment photos — images you choose to upload are stored securely and associated with your account.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No data from third-party sources

How your data is stored

All data is stored on Supabase, a hosted database platform. Your content is scoped to your account and is not accessible to other users. Garment images are stored in Supabase Storage. Supabase's infrastructure is hosted on AWS.

How your data is used

Your data is used exclusively to power the TrailLayers app for your own use. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

Deleting your data

You can delete all your data or your entire account at any time from within the app under Profile → Account → Delete All Data or Delete Account. Deleting your account permanently removes all associated data from our systems.

Children's privacy

TrailLayers is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to TrailLayers@proton.me.
